# AI Tafsir - Design Prompts

Use these prompts with your image generation tool (e.g., Google Nano Banana, Midjourney, DALL-E 3) to create consistent and high-quality assets for the project.

## Brand Identity
**Theme**: Modern, Spiritual, Technological, Clean.
**Colors**: Emerald Green (`#10b981`), Deep Slate (`#0f172a`), White.

---

### 1. Main Logo
**Usage**: Header, Landing Page, Branding.
**Prompt**:
> A professional, modern logo for an application named "AI Tafsir". The design should elegantly fuse the silhouette of an open book (representing the Quran) with a subtle, glowing digital node or neural network connection point (representing AI). The style should be flat vector art, minimalist, and clean. Use a primary color of emerald green on a white background. The design must be legible at small sizes.

### 2. App Icon / Favicon
**Usage**: Browser Tab, PWA Icon, Mobile Home Screen.
**Prompt**:
> A simple, high-contrast app icon. An abstract, stylized emerald green letter 'Q' or an open book symbol that transforms into a digital pixel array at the edge. Minimalist, flat vector style. Centered on a white background with ample padding. No text.

### 3. OpenGraph / Social Preview Image
**Usage**: Link previews on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn.
**Prompt**:
> A cinematic, high-resolution social media preview image for "AI Tafsir". The composition features a sleek, futuristic glass tablet displaying Arabic calligraphy, floating in a dark void illuminated by soft emerald green data streams. In the center, the text "AI Tafsir" appears in a modern, bold sans-serif font. The background features subtle, dark Islamic geometric patterns. Professional, tech-forward, and spiritual atmosphere. Aspect ratio 1.91:1.

### 4. PWA Splash Screen Background
**Usage**: Mobile app startup screen.
**Prompt**:
> A vertical mobile wallpaper background. A deep slate blue-black gradient background. In the center, a faint, glowing emerald green Islamic geometric rosette pattern constructed from thin, high-tech circuit lines. The design should be symmetrical, calming, and abstract. No text.

### 5. Empty State Illustration (Search)
**Usage**: Displayed when the search results are empty.
**Prompt**:
> A flat, modern vector illustration for a "No Results" state. A stylized magnifying glass hovering over an open book, with a small question mark. The style should be friendly and clean, using soft greys and emerald green accents. White background.

### 6. Hero Banner Background
**Usage**: Background for the main landing area or header.
**Prompt**:
> A wide, abstract background texture. Soft, flowing waves of emerald green light moving across a dark slate canvas. The waves should look like digital data streams or silk. Very low contrast, meant to sit behind white text. Modern, abstract, and clean.
